Day Job

Human Rights Foundation

Serving as Senior Director of Operations at the Human Rights Foundation, leading capacity building, finance, and events. I'm the executive producer of the Oslo Freedom Forum — one of the world's most important gatherings for human rights defenders and dissidents.

In 2024, I co-founded the Finney Freedom Prize — an award honoring individuals who advance financial freedom and human rights through Bitcoin, named after the legendary cryptographer Hal Finney.

The through-line between Bitcoin and human rights has never been clearer to me. Freedom of money is a prerequisite for freedom of everything else.
